The Audi A5's safety features include dual front, front-side and curtain airbags. Front occupants allso get knee airbags. There is an active rollover protection system in place to help stop or reduce any damage to the occupands in case the vehicle rolls over. On the premium plus version, a blind spot warning is available, as well as adaptive cruise control.
